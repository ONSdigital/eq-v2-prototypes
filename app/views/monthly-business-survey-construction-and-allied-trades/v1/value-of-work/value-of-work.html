This survey requires you to report the value of work

Value of work refers to a precise valuation or informed estimate of chargeable work carried out during the reporting period. This includes work started, work in progress and work completed.

Include:

  • work done by your own business on its own business premises
  • fixtures, equipment and tools your business made and used in construction
  • materials your business used, overheads and profits
  • labour costs on your payroll

  • value of work done by subcontractors
