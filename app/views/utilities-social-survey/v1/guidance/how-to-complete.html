How to complete this survey

What you need to do

You be asked to provide specific values for the amount you have paid in a reporting period for:

  • Energy utilities - gas, electric and water
  • Broadband, telephone and TV
  • Household shopping such as groceries

We will then summarise this information and calculate your total spend for the reporting period and ask you to confirm it is correct.
