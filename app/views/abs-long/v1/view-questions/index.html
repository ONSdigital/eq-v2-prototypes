How to complete and reporting period

Question
Are you able to report figures for the period 1 May 2016 to 31 May 2016?

Traded for part of the year?

Please select 'No, I need to report for a different period'

Cannot report for this calendar year?

Please select 'No, I need to report for a different period'


You can answer with the following options:

  • Yes, I can report for this period
  • No, I need to report for a different period
What dates will you be reporting for?

Only traded for a part of the year?

Please provide figures for the period in which you were trading.

Only commenced trading during 2021?

Your return should cover the period from the commencement of your business until 31 December 2021 or, alternatively, any date up to 5 April 2022.

Ceased trading during 2021?

Your return should cover the period 1 January 2021 to the date you ceased to trade or, alternatively, from the beginning of your last business year up to the cessation date.


You will have to enter a 'from' and 'to' input for the dates you are reporting.

Income

Question Guidance
For the period 1 May 2016 to 31 May 2016, what was ESSENTIAL ENTERPRISE LTD.'s total turnover?

Include:

  • progress payments on work in progress on long-term contracts
  • commission paid

Exclude:

  • full value of sales if only part payment in commission has been received
  • VAT

Enter a value to the nearest £thousand (e.g. 56,000). Do not enter ‘56’ for £56,000.
Does your business produce goods or services that protect the environment?

Include:

Goods and/or services that protect the environment that are produced by your business. For example:

  • production or installation of solar panels
  • waste collection, treatment and disposal services
  • environmental consultancy and training services

Exclude:

Goods and/or services that protect the environment that are used by your business. For example:

  • the use of energy saving light bulbs
  • the consumption of recycled materials

You can answer with the following options:

  • Yes, I can report for this period
  • No, I need to report for a different period
Approximately what percentage related to the production of environmental goods or services?

You can answer with the following options:

  • 75-100%
  • 50-74%
  • 25-49%
  • 10-24%
  • 0-9%
