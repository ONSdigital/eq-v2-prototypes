For the reporting period, what was your expenditure on Research and Development (R&D) in relation to the offshore wind sector?

Guidance to help to answer this question

Research and Development (R&D) covers basic and applied research and development. It covers creative and systematic work undertaken to increase knowledge or to devise new applications of available knowledge. R&D activity must meet all five of these criteria: novel, creative, uncertain, systematic, transferable/reproducible.

What we mean by the five criteria

  • Novel: aimed at new findings
  • Creative: based on original, not obvious, concepts and hypotheses
  • Uncertain: uncertain about the final outcome
  • Systematic: planned and budgeted
  • Transferable/reproducible: to lead to results that could possibly be reproduced
